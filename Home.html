Restaurant Description

Serving burgers since 1942, Brian's is a staple here in the city. Nominated for over
1,000 Foodie Awards and winning 876 of those! We use only locally grown
sustainable ingredients. From burgers to dogs, sausage to brisket, Brian's Burgers
is a choice you cannot go wrong with! Bring the kids and enjoy free ice cream
for 12 and under! Dining options include a scenic overlook of the ocean or
the rustic atmosphere of our spacious dining hall.
